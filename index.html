Spring rolls

Hi, thank you for visiting my first trial responsive page. In this section, I'd like to show you guys how I make Vietnamese spring rolls.

Before we start, let go through the ingredients

  • Rice paper
  • Shrimps
  • Pork meat
  • Egg
  • Different type of salad, sweet basil, bean sprout
  • Rice noodle
  • Hoisin sauce, chilli and garlic

Typically, Vietnamese uses hands to eat spring roll. This is a compact food, which is healthy, light, goes with slavory home-made hoisin sauce.

Cook shrimps and pork well. Fry the egg and cut to small pieces. Noodle also cook well. Then wet the rice paper, start to place all ingredients nicely. Try to roll as neat as you can. First it can be trick. Once you master it, then I will be proud. That's it. You have your perfect rolls.

Making sauce is an art. Because I can not tell exactly how much flavor should you pour in the sauce. Let's trust your gut. So, boil hoisin paste with some water, add little fish sauce, fried garlic and chilli. Here you go, nice sauce. Enjoy. Thanks
